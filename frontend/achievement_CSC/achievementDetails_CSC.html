Triumph as Champions

Congratulations to Team Null for their remarkable achievement in securing the Championship title at the FlutterFlow Developer Group HackFest: Impact Challenge.

The event took place at Google Philippines, BGC, Taguig, and Common Ground, Rockwell, Makati on November 22-23, 2024. Team Null developed an innovative app that promotes balanced and healthier eating habits using advanced photo analysis and city-wide nutrition trends.

Their creativity and ability to build meaningful, impactful solutions continue to inspire and motivate those around them. Keep shining, Team Null!
